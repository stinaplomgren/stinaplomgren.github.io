Week 6: Casting

A Work in Progress

Design

I decided that I wanted to cast a ring in bronze after talking to the TA at the metal shop on campus. He said it wouldn't be too hard and that I needed to get a 3D print of the ring that I wanted to make. I designed this ring in TinkerCAD using Codeblocks, as it was much easier to manipulate the Codeblocks into doing what I wanted them to do. I chose the phrase וארשתיך לי לעום from Hosea 2.

Casting
