Week 12: Final Project

Plant Stand Realized

Assembly

This was the easiest and most fun part of the project. I assembled the physical structure of the shelf with some wood that I got from Home Depot as well as the brackets that I 3D printed. If you want a close up you can refer to my TinkerCAD design in Week 1. Pretty straightforward.

Programming

This part was a bit more complicated. I had figured out how to program the Bluetooth to Raspberry Pi connection back in Week 7, but I wanted to take it a step further. I decided that since I didn't want to connect all the speakers in a network, that I should have the raspberry pi start playing music as soon as I plug it in. I got the inspiration from our Christmas tree: it gives light as soon as I plug it in, and I wanted music as soon as I pluged in the Pi. I coded this, and some code can be found on my repository.

Final Results

Here is my shelf with some grow lights attached to it. It's pretty cool and my plants appreciate the music.

Notes:

  • I was a little surprised with how much time it took to program the Pi. I guess I shouldn't have been surprised given how much time it took me in Week 7, but it did.
